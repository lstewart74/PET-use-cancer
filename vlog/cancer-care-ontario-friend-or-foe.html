Cancer Care Ontario - Friend or Foe?

Vlog Archive

Dr. David Webster 4 April, 2018

Cancer Care Ontario in theory is supposed to determine, based on the evidence, what would be the most appropriate investigations and treatments for cancer patients. In addition, they assess all indications for PET/CT such as for investigation of dementia, cardiac and many other uses. Based on the video and documents you can decide for yourselves whether they are doing “just what is best for Ontario’s cancer patients”, or acting as “mouthpieces for Liberal Health Care Policy.” And if they have spent all this time, money and effort to block Ontario cancer patients from the accepted world standard of imaging management of cancer for up 20 years, just what else have they done to Ontario’s cancer patients on behalf of the Liberal government?
